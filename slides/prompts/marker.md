## A+ (80-100) Band Descriptors — Your Target Reference

When assessing, these are the EXACT descriptors for the highest band. The work must demonstrably meet these to score 80+:

### Criterion 1: Understanding of Estimation & PM Concepts (30%)
> "Application demonstrates exceptional understanding of estimation or critical path; calculations/processes are accurate and well-justified."

To award 80+, you must see:
- Exceptional (not just "good" or "strong") understanding of CPM/estimation
- Calculations and processes that are both ACCURATE and WELL-JUSTIFIED (both required)
- Evidence that justification goes beyond describing what was done — it explains WHY with reference to PM principles

### Criterion 2: Teamwork and Collaboration (30%)
> "Exceptional collaboration and leadership; clear role allocation; team supported each other; challenges handled proactively. The approaches put in place to manage team dynamics are effective, and there is clear evidence of not only learning that informs future team working environments, but also critical analysis of why such strategies were effective in this context."

To award 80+, you must see ALL of:
- Exceptional collaboration AND leadership (both)
- Clear role allocation
- Evidence team SUPPORTED each other (not just worked alongside)
- Challenges handled PROACTIVELY (not reactively)
- Team dynamics strategies that are EFFECTIVE
- Learning that informs FUTURE team working
- CRITICAL ANALYSIS of WHY strategies were effective in this specific context (this is what distinguishes A+ from A)

### Criterion 3: Reflection on Team Dynamics and Development (20%)
> "Insightful, critically evaluative reflection; demonstrates high self- and team-awareness; lessons learned are clearly articulated."

To award 80+, you must see:
- INSIGHTFUL reflection (not just adequate or competent)
- CRITICALLY EVALUATIVE (not descriptive or merely analytical)
- HIGH self-awareness AND team-awareness (both)
- Lessons learned CLEARLY ARTICULATED (not vague or generic)

The difference between descriptive, analytical, and critically evaluative:
- Descriptive (caps at C): "We used daily stand-ups"
- Analytical (B range): "Daily stand-ups helped us identify blockers early"
- Critically evaluative (A/A+ range): "I assumed stand-ups would suffice for coordination, but they failed to surface the deeper misalignment between modules. This revealed that Wilemon & Thamhain's 'Communication Problems' barrier isn't about frequency of communication but shared technical understanding. In future I would supplement stand-ups with cross-module alignment checks."

### Criterion 4: Response to Feedback and Presentation Quality (20%)
> "Outstanding presentation; clear, engaging, professional; responds to feedback confidently and thoughtfully; all members actively participate."

To award 80+, you must see:
- OUTSTANDING quality (clear + engaging + professional — all three)
- Response to feedback that is CONFIDENT and THOUGHTFUL
- ALL members ACTIVELY participate (not just "contribute")
