## Speaker Script

"Good morning. We are Group [X] and today we will present our Critical Path Method application — a temporary endeavour with a defined beginning and end, aimed at creating a unique product. I am [A], I led the algorithm development — taking the Specialist role in Belbin's terms. [B] led the JSON parser and data model as our Implementer. [C] managed testing and quality assurance as Completer Finisher. And [D] built the visualization and coordinated integration as our Co-ordinator. We allocated roles based on individual strengths and used a RACI framework to ensure clear accountability across the team. Our primary stakeholders were the academic panel — high power, high interest on Mendelow's matrix — which meant we prioritised demonstrating PM understanding throughout, not just technical accuracy. We chose to automate critical path identification because the algorithm — with its forward pass, backward pass, and convergence handling — presented a rich opportunity to demonstrate PM scheduling and estimation concepts in practice. We adopted an iterative approach suited to our short timeline and uncertain task durations, managing this as a structured digital project from the outset."
