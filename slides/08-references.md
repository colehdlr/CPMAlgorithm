## Formatting Rules (Harvard Style)

- **Books:** Author, A.B. (Year) *Title in Italics*. Edition. Place: Publisher.
- **Journal articles:** Author, A.B. (Year) 'Article title', *Journal Name in Italics*, Volume(Issue), pp. X-Y.
- **Websites:** Author/Organisation (Year) *Title*. Available at: URL (Accessed: Day Month Year).
- Alphabetical order by first author surname
- Hanging indent for entries longer than one line
