Post-lecture Activity
• Organise your group reflection
• For the 2nd intervention your focus is: 
• How did your team approach planning and estimation?
• Did everyone contribute equally? Why/why not?
• Have there been disagreements? How were they handled? 
• If you have not had disagreements, what practices have you put in place to minimise 
conflict?
• Are you communicating effectively? 
• Are you working ethically and professionally?
